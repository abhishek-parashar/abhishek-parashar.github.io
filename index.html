ABOUT ME

A very warm welcome to my share of internet!!! I'm a data science research enthusiast, Google AI Explore ML Facilitator and a DRDO research intern. I've a deep rooted interest for mathematics, physics and Data science in general.

Apart from consuming delicacies, I like to play guitar, percussion instruments, keyboard, shake some legs on chartbusters and play cricket. Sometimes, I enter a world of my own and usually bring that to paper in form of poetry and stories :D

Since last couple of years, I’ve been in the machine learning and deep learning domain. I’ve explored a great deal of learning models and a lot of computational mathematics. After exploring these fundamentals behind AI and it’s sub-disciplines, I figured there’s a need for some external source of information to solve out this mystery of mathematics and learning. Thus, I continue to research to elucidate the nebulous of jargon existing in the field of Machine Learning.

Deep Learning Research Intern

My aim was to collect EEG signals (give information about brain activity), pre-process them and then predict the cognitive ability of the person by using neural networks. I developed a GUI software that predicts that the subject is confused or not based on his/her cognitive ability. The data was collected using EEG cathode cap, for preprocessing I used python frameworks numpy and pandas. After verifying and trying many algorithms, I used the Gradient Boosting Algorithm. The result were far better than fellow researchers working on same data set available at Kaggle. I continue to research on various topics of Deep learning with DRDO and I also represented DRDO at a conference of importance
